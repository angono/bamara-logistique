À Propos de Nous

LOGISTIQUE-BAMARA-LOGISTICS est une société unipersonnelle à responsabilité limitée de droit Centrafricain, créée en 2017. Nos objectifs sont de fournir des solutions de logistique de proximité, offrir de l'emploi aux jeunes à travers nos prestations, et trouver des solutions logistiques aux problèmes liés à l'environnement.

Company Profile: LOGISTIQUE-BAMARA-LOGISTICS

About Us: Established in 2017 and headquartered in Bangui, Central African Republic (CAR), LOGISTIQUE-BAMARA-LOGISTICS is a dynamic company specializing in proximity logistics, infrastructure management, and tailored business services. Committed to excellence, we deliver secure, efficient, and innovative solutions while actively contributing to youth empowerment through job creation.

Our Mission: To provide reliable, secure, and rapid logistics services that meet the evolving needs of businesses and individuals. We prioritize quality, safety, and customer satisfaction, alongside fostering sustainable development by creating employment opportunities for young professionals in CAR.
